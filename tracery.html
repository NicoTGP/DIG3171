Video games have cemented themselves in the modern era and are unavoidable in this day and age. It is placed on par with such media as film, books, music, etc. An interactive art form that combines images, music and everything in between to offer a unique past time/experience. Articles like this href to highlight how video games, despite having been around for less than half the time as film, still aren't treated or viewed as pieces of art and cultural cornerstones, especially in the modern digital age.
